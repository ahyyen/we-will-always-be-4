Jullien: Nightmares

For a while, I never slept more than four hours a night. Sometimes I just had so many thoughts and memories swirling around in my mind that I couldn't fall asleep at all. Other times I finally did manage to fall asleep after a long while, only to find myself suddenly awake again, out of breath, cold sweat coating my forehead, eyes frantically darting around my room in fear.

I was so tired all the damn time. I was sick of it. I almost gave up, if I'm being honest. Haha.

But never again! I'm never giving up again. I won't let anyone else feel the way I did. Especially them three.
