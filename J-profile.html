Jullien Lyken

Age: 19

Pronouns: he/him

Birthday: June 17

Roles: magician, actor

According to Jullien, being a magician is just another form of acting. If you don't believe in the tricks you're performing, then your audience will see right through you. Being a magician isn't just about performing tricks - it's about playing a character, embodying the role of a magician.

Jullien grew up near his local theater, and so was surrounded by all kinds of shows from an early age. He was particularly fascinated by magic shows and musicals, which then inspired his own passion for performing. He is now a second-year theater student at the renown Oak Valley Conservatory to pursue this passion (what he really wanted was to be a magician but unfortunately such a specific department didn't exist). Outside of class he often goes out to perform little shows on the city streets, which tends to attract a lot of local children. But, really, his shows are for audiences of all ages. That was how he met Swift, who he then introduced to Hallie.
