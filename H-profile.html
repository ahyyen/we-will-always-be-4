Hallie Corvera

Age: 22

Pronouns: she/they

Birthday: February 14

Roles: songwriter, singer

Hallie was born with defective lungs. A cruel fate for someone so naturally energetic, daring, and outgoing. Someone who, above all, loved to sing. But Hallie was determined not to give up on her passion. Ever since she watched the characters in kids' shows sing their silly little songs, she knew she wanted to do the same. Even though she couldn't sing in the loud, powerful way she wanted to, she was determined to let the world hear her music. So Hallie sang.

At first, however, it was more out of spite than anything else. Young Hallie cursed the universe for taking away the one thing she loved the most. She kept singing and singing, desperate to prove the universe wrong, desperate to defy her own fate. Then, one day, her lungs gave out and she collapsed. Laying in a hospital bed had a way of making one contemplate their life carefully. Hallie realized she had turned something she loved into a source of her hate, forgetting what inspired her to sing. She begged her younger self to forgive her, and when she was discharged from the hospital she began singing. For real this time.

Despite all odds, Hallie ended up making it into Oak Valley Conservatory's music division. Eventually, by pure chance, she ran into Jullien, a theater student two years her junior.
