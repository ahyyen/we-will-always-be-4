Jullien: Karaoke

So we all know Hallie can't exert herself too much, so our options for where to hang out are limited. A lot of times we just go to her apartment, since that's where she's most comfortable and also where all her medical supplies are in case anything happens. But we could tell Hallie was getting bored of being cooped up in that house all the time, even if we were all there with her. So, after some discussion, me and Swift and Theo put our money together to buy a karaoke machine for her.

She loved it! The day we brought it over to her place we ended up singing until 2am (we got a noise complaint from the people next door so we had to stop haha oops). We all slept over that night, and when I woke up the next morning I noticed Hallie, still sleeping, had her hand around one of the karaoke microphones.

We still have karaoke sessions at Hallie's every now and then. She even started to give the other two some vocal coaching during those sessions. She really does love singing.
