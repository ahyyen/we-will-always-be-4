Swift Riviera

Age: 19

Pronouns: she/her

Birthday: March 6

Roles: dancer, acrobat

Hesper Rae was born to a family of scientists. She and her two siblings were each expected to carry on that legacy. Her siblings gladly obliged; she refused to do the same. She rejected everything to do with her family, including the name they gave her.

But doing so left her completely empty. She had no dreams, no goals, no plan for her future aside from a vague "not scientist". She often found herself aimlessly wandering through the city streets, not knowing what she was searching for. One day, when she was thirteen years old, she came across a ballet performance on an outdoor stage at a park. Instantly, she fell in love with the elegance and beauty of the dance, unable to take her eyes away for the entire show. That evening, she managed to strike a deal with her parents: if they let her take ballet lessons, she would study to become a scientist like they always wanted.

Several years down the line, she discovered an interest in acrobatics as well, which her ballet teacher encouraged her to explore. That was how she met a fellow aspiring acrobat named Theo. Eventually they met two other young performers, and through her new friends, Swift finally found her new name.
