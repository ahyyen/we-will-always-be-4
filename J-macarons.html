Jullien: Macarons

Did you know that, before we met, none of the others had ever tried macarons before? I say that’s simply unacceptable! Before I realized it myself, I had dragged all three of them to the bakery. Swift instantly fell in love with how cute and colorful the macarons were. Theo was hesitant at first (he doesn’t like sweets usually) but he eventually softened up to the coffee-flavored one. And judging by the way Hallie instantly went back to buy a whole box after finishing hers, I say that trip was a huge success, hehehe~

…It’s funny how precious the small things can be, huh?
