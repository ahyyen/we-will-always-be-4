Theo: About Jullien

It took us a while to realize that, from the moment we first met Jullien, he was already acting. That he was so much more than the carefree, optimistic, silly attitude he constantly displayed. I’m glad he’s able to reveal more of his true self to us now, but sometimes, I think it is only when he is on a stage, reciting somebody else’s words, that his mask truly comes off.
