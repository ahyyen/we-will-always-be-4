Theodor Vox

Age: 23

Pronouns: he/him

Birthday: September 20

Roles: acrobat, dancer

Theo is responsible, mature, calm, and dedicated. Everyone looks up to him and sees him as a natural leader when they first meet him. But what they don't realize is the sheer amount of work it took to become the person he is today.

Theo and his older brother Benjamin were raised by a single father, who was rarely at home because of work. Theo grew up as an outcast, and he quickly learned to suppress his emotions to help him attract as little attention as possible. Of course, it hurt him to do so all the time, leading to violent outbursts of anger any time something even mildly annoyed him. These outbursts escalated to the point where he had to be expelled from high school.

If Theo hadn't happened to stumble across an acrobatics performance at a local fair that summer, he might have continued down his dark path. Luckily, the performance captivated him, and he eventually found some lessons that he could afford. A couple years later he met a dancer who was just beginning to get into acrobatics. Her name was Hesper.
