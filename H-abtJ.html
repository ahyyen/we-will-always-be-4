Hallie: About Jullien

The issue with Jullien being an actor: that little shit is way too good at poker! He's the only person I've yet to win a single game against. But if that's not enough, his magician skills have made him amazing at handling cards! So there's no way he'd ever accidentally slip up and let me sneak a peek at his cards. Not that I'd ever do such a thing anyway. I play honorably, thank you very much!

Growing up, a lot of people told me I was too enthusiastic about things I liked, that I should chill out or whatever. They don't understand that I might not get an entire life to do what I want to do. So I'm just glad Jullien is always down to play no matter how many times I challenge him to a poker game.
