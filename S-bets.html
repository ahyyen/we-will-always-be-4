Swift: Bets

There's this really dumb game that Hallie started between the four of us. Here's how it goes: one person bets against another person, and whoever wins has to then bet against a different person, and it just keeps going. The only rule is that the bets have to be relatively small and inconsequential. For example, once Theo bet $10 that I would not be able to finish eating an plate of pasta while staying en pointe. I have to say he greatly underestimated my capabilities.

The last bet was between me and Hallie. I bet $15 that I could beat her in a game of blackjack. She was absolutely delighted that she now had an excuse to play cards again.

...Of course, that game never happened. I guess I owe Hallie $15.
