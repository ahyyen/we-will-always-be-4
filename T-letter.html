Theo: A Letter Unsent

Dear Benjamin,

I wish you could meet my friends. I think you’d like them a lot. There’s this cheerful, headstrong boy named Jullien, who has honestly said some of the most hilarious, out-of-pocket things I’ve ever heard. Then there’s Swift, the one who introduced me to dance, and also loves muffins as much as you did. And Hallie…yeah. There’s three of them. It’s not wrong to call them my saviors. I think, maybe, they could have been yours, too.

With love,

Your younger brother, Theo
